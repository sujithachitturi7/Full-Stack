CHHATRAPATI SHIVAJI MAHARAJ

Founder of the Maratha Empire

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was a fearless warrior king who founded the Maratha Empire and became a symbol of courage, justice, and strong leadership in Indian history.

Overview about Shivaji Maharaj

  • Birth : Shivaji was born on 19 February 1630 in Shivneri Fort.
  • Parents: His father was Shahaji Bhosale and his mother was Jijabai. His mother inspired him with stories of bravery and dharma.
  • Founder of Maratha Empire: Shivaji founded the Maratha Empire and became a great king.
  • Coronation: He was crowned as king in 1674 at Raigad Fort.
  • Guerrilla Warfare: Shivaji used guerrilla warfare tactics, meaning surprise attacks and quick movements, to defeat stronger enemies.
  • Forts Control: He captured and controlled many forts in Maharashtra such as Torna Fort and Sinhagad Fort.
  • Good Administration : Shivaji created a strong administration system called Ashta Pradhan to help run the kingdom.
  • Respect for Women and Religion: He respected women and people of all religions and treated them fairly.
  • Strong Navy: Shivaji built one of the first strong Indian naval forces to protect the coastline.
  • Death: Shivaji Maharaj died on 3 April 1680 at Raigad Fort.
